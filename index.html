The JIIT Innovation Hub is a space where creativity meets technology. We empower students and innovators with labs, mentorship, and resources to turn ideas into real-world solutions, fostering a strong culture of research and entrepreneurship.

Ride Hack 2025

48-Hour Innovation Challenge

NOV 1 2025

About Ride Hack 2025

Ride Hack 2025 is the flagship hackathon of the JIIT Innovation Hub, designed to bring together creative minds to solve real-world problems through technology and innovation. This 48-hour intensive coding competition will challenge students, developers, designers, and entrepreneurs to create innovative solutions under the theme of "Smart Mobility and Sustainable Transportation".

Event Schedule

Day 1 - November 1, 2025

  • 09:00 AM Registration & Breakfast
  • 10:00 AM Inauguration & Theme Announcement
  • 11:00 AM Team Formation & Idea Pitching
  • 01:00 PM Lunch Break
  • 02:00 PM Hacking Begins
  • 06:00 PM Mentorship Session
  • 09:00 PM Dinner

Day 2 - November 2, 2025

  • 08:00 AM Breakfast
  • 09:00 AM Hacking Continues
  • 01:00 PM Lunch Break
  • 02:00 PM Project Submission Deadline
  • 03:00 PM Presentations & Judging
  • 06:00 PM Prize Distribution & Closing Ceremony
